Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 June 2026

The short version: We collect your email address and the details you type into our tools so we can generate your letter/contract and send you a copy. We don't sell your data. We use trusted third parties (listed below) to send emails and process AI generation. You can ask us to delete your data at any time.

1. Who We Are

This Privacy Policy explains how GetPaidTrade ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use getpaidtrade.co.uk (the "Service"). We are the data controller for the purposes of UK GDPR.

2. What We Collect

When you use our free or paid tools, we collect:

  • Email address — so we can send you your generated letter/contract and account-related communications
  • Name and business details — if you provide them, used to personalise generated content
  • Job/dispute details you type in — customer names, amounts owed, job descriptions — used solely to generate your requested document
  • Payment information — handled entirely by our payment processor (LemonSqueezy); we never see or store your card details
  • Basic usage data — pages visited, via Google Search Console, to understand how the Service is used

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To generate the letter, contract, or other document you've requested
  • To send you a copy of generated content via email
  • To send occasional emails about new tools or features relevant to you — you can unsubscribe at any time
  • To process payments and manage your subscription, if you're a GetPaidTrade Pro subscriber
  • To improve the Service based on aggregate, anonymised usage patterns

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third Parties We Use

To provide the Service, we share data with the following trusted processors:

ProviderPurpose
Anthropic (Claude API)Generates the text of your letter/contract based on the details you provide
ResendSends transactional emails (your generated letter, welcome emails, access links)

Each of these providers processes data only as necessary to perform their specific function and is bound by their own privacy and data protection obligations.

5. How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain your email address and account data for as long as you have an active relationship with GetPaidTrade, and for a reasonable period afterward in case you return. You can request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 7).

Job/dispute details entered into our tools (customer names, amounts, descriptions) are used to generate your document in real time and are not permanently stored by us beyond what's necessary to deliver your generated content via email.

6. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Withdraw consent to marketing emails at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) if you believe your data has been mishandled

7. How to Exercise Your Rights

To access, correct, or delete your data, or to unsubscribe from emails, contact us at hello@getpaidtrade.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days as required by law.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and secure third-party infrastructure, to protect your data. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your information.

9. Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary for the Service to function and basic analytics via Google Search Console to understand site performance. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or trackers.

10. Children's Privacy

The Service is intended for business use by adults operating as tradespeople or small business owners. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or a notice on the Service.

12. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy or your data can be sent to hello@getpaidtrade.co.uk.
